Creating Healthy Smiles in Fairfax, VA

At Ideal Family Smile, we give you a smile you can be proud of! Our friendly dentists and team love helping take smiles to the next level, and we will work one-on-one with you to design your treatment plan. Call us today to schedule an appointment and learn more about dental care in Fairfax, VA!

We accept most insurances and we accept cash, check, and credit cards.

Our Services

Dental Implants

Do you have missing teeth? Dental implants are a long-term solution for restoring your smile!

Dental Veneers

Are your teeth cracked, chipped, have stains or other imperfections? Dental Veneers can give you the smile you're looking for!

Dental Crowns

Crowns are used to cover and protect the shape of your teeth when fillings don’t solve the problem.
