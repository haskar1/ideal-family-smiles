Privacy Policy

Ideal Family Smiles, LLC (“IFS”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) takes the privacy of our patients seriously. This Privacy Policy describes the types of personal information we collect, how we use and protect that information, and with whom we may share it.

Information Collection and Use

IFS collects personal information from patients when they schedule appointments, receive treatment, or complete forms on our website. The types of information we collect may include names, addresses, telephone numbers, email addresses, insurance information, and medical history. We use this information to provide dental services, communicate with patients, and comply with legal requirements.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

IFS may share personal information with third parties as necessary to provide dental services, such as sharing information with dental insurance companies or laboratories that create dental restorations. We may also share information as required by law, such as in response to a court order or subpoena.

Data Security

IFS takes reasonable steps to protect personal information from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We use a combination of physical and electronic security measures, such as secure servers, firewalls, and encryption, to protect personal information.

Data Retention

IFS will retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide dental services, comply with legal requirements, or resolve disputes.

Your Choices and Rights

Patients have the right to request access to their personal information, request that their personal information be corrected or deleted, and request that we limit the use of their personal information. Patients also have the right to opt-out of receiving marketing communications from IFS.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

IFS may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify patients of any changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on our website.
