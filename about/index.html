About Us

At Ideal Family Smiles, our experienced dentists provide comprehensive care to people of all ages! We work with you to create the ideal treatment plan that will achieve your goals.

Customer service is our top priority. We make sure we address your dental care needs and that you are happy with the results. You can count on us to give you a healthier, happier smile!

We accept most insurances and we accept cash, check, and card.

Our Team

Dr. Yaser Nasif, DDS

For over 20 years, Dr. Yaser Nasif has been practicing dentistry in multiple countries, including the United Arab Emirates, Syria and seven years in the United States. Back in 2000, he graduated from Damascus University School of Dentistry, where he completed his Advanced Education in General Dentistry residency at the Lutheran Medical Center.

Dr. Nasif provides general dentistry services and is big on dental implants to help reach his goal of putting a smile on patients’ faces. He has a diploma in implant dentistry from the American Academy of Implant Dentistry and is a member of the American Dental Association® . To this day, he continues his education and training every six months.

Outside of work, Dr. Nasif loves spending time with his wife and four kids. He enjoys traveling and reading whenever he can.

Dr. Asad Ansari, DDS

Dr. Asad Ansari, a Virginia native, has been living in the DMV area since 2005. He attended Virginia Tech where he earned his undergraduate degree in biochemistry. He then received his doctorate from New York University College of Dentistry.

Licensed in Washington DC and Virginia, Dr. Ansari is an enthusiastic and compassionate general dentist who enjoys treating patients of all ages. His priority has always been providing comprehensive, high-quality care to patients in a relaxed and comfortable atmosphere. In his free time, Dr. Ansari likes to spend time outdoors, hiking, archery and loves to travel and spend time with family and friends.
