Dental Fillings

Our dentists may recommend dental fillings to restore the health of your teeth if they have developed cavities. Fillings can be completed comfortably in just one visit to Ideal Family Dentistry so that you can regain your smile and return to your regular activities. Call us at 804-406-9595 to schedule an appointment with our fantastic team of dentists and learn more about dental fillings in Fairfax, Virginia.

Dental fillings are simple restorations used to repair teeth that have sustained minor to moderate damage, such as that caused by a cavity or by minor fracturing and chipping. Fillings work by literally “filling in” the damaged portions of the tooth, restoring its original structure and function to return it to good health. In the past, dental fillings have been made using a silver amalgam material, but composite (tooth-colored) fillings are more commonly used today.

Fillings can be placed in just one appointment. When you receive a filling, our dentists will remove the damaged tooth tissue and thoroughly clean the remaining tooth to remove all traces of decay. The filling material will then be placed and shaped to match the contours of the tooth. We will finish your treatment by hardening the filling and polishing the tooth to give you a long-lasting solution for your smile.

Our dentists and team will check for signs of tooth decay or other tooth damage at each of your regular appointments. If damage is found, we will carefully evaluate the tooth to determine if a filling is needed to restore it.
