Dental Implants

Missing teeth? Our dedicated team of dentists can provide you with dental implants! Implants are a long-term solution for your smile, replacing both the crowns and the roots of your teeth to restore your smile and oral function. We welcome you to call Ideal Family Dentistry at 804-406-9595 to learn more about dental implants in Fairfax, Virginia, and schedule your consultation with our experienced dentists.

Dental implants are prosthetic teeth designed to replace your missing teeth and provide a long-term solution for your smile and oral health. Implants may be used to replace one tooth, several teeth or an entire arch of teeth. Implants consist of two main parts, an implant post and an implant restoration.

The implant post is a small screw, made of titanium or another biocompatible material, which replaces the tooth root. After being surgically placed into the jawbone, the implant post integrates with your bone tissue to provide a stable foundation for your tooth. It performs all the same functions as your original tooth root in stimulating your supporting bone to keep your mouth healthy. Implant restorations are designed to cap the implant post, fully restoring your smile and completing your treatment. Depending on your individual needs, your implant restoration could be a single crown, a bridge or a denture. Our dentists will take care to help ensure that your implant restoration is designed to fit your mouth perfectly and provide you with a beautiful result.

It is crucial that you replace missing teeth as soon as possible. Missing teeth can cause your supporting bone tissue to deteriorate, leading to problems with your bite and misalignment of your remaining teeth. Implants are considered the optimal replacement for your missing teeth as they look, feel and function exactly like your original teeth and, with proper oral hygiene care, can last a lifetime.
