Emergency Dentistry

Having a dental emergency? Ideal Family Smile can help! Our dentists and team understand that accidents happen, and we provide emergency dental care in Fairfax, Virginia, to help you receive treatment and regain a healthy smile as quickly as possible. If you experience a dental emergency, call us at 804-406-9595 immediately. Our team will provide you with instructions on what to do and make arrangements for you to meet with our fantastic team of dentists at the soonest available time.

A dental emergency is any type of situation that requires immediate attention from a dentist. Our team understands that dental emergencies are rarely convenient, and we will work to accommodate your needs and schedule when you contact us about a dental emergency. We recommend that you contact us as soon as possible if you experience a dental emergency, as time can sometimes be a factor in treating certain types of injuries. We will schedule an emergency appointment with you and provide any instructions you might need to care for your mouth and smile until you can meet with our dentists and receive professional care.

Common dental emergencies include:

  • Tooth pain, including severe, lasting or chronic toothaches
  • Lost dental restorations, such as lost or broken fillings or crowns
  • Damage to the soft oral tissues (lips, tongue, cheeks, gums, etc.)
  • Cracked or fractured teeth
  • Chipped teeth
  • Partially or fully knocked-out teeth

If you experience trauma to your head or neck, or if your injury may be life threatening, we advise that you call 911 or visit the nearest emergency room to seek medical treatment, even if you may also require dental care.
